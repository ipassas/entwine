01 — Logo

The wordmark

A single SVG wordmark, recoloured with currentColor. Use white on dark backgrounds and ink (#0A0A0A) on light. Keep clear space of at least the cap-height around it; never stretch or recolour outside the palette.

White — on ink / black / photography.

Ink — on light surfaces (#F8F8F8 / #F1F2F4 / white).

06 — Motion

Motion

Sections fade + rise (26px → 0) on scroll-in over ~0.8s on an ease-out curve, with a light 80ms stagger across grid children. Buttons lift 1px on hover. All motion is disabled under prefers-reduced-motion.
