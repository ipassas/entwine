For Restaurants

Your wine programme has more potential than you think. Entwine helps you realise it — and gives every guest their own sommelier at the table.

From inventory management and fast wine ordering to restaurant-specific pairings that delight your guests — Entwine is the wine intelligence platform built for the way restaurants actually work.

See the Platform → Book a Demo

Sound familiar?

Wine ordering takes too long
Chasing suppliers, checking stock levels, reconciling deliveries against what was ordered — all manual, all time-consuming.
No visibility on what's actually working
You have a wine list. You have sales data somewhere. But turning that into actionable intelligence about your programme? That's a spreadsheet nightmare.
Guests are choosing wine by price, not pairing
Without guidance, most diners default to the second-cheapest bottle. The right pairing recommendation changes that — and changes the bill.
Your team knows the menu — but not the wine
Front-of-house staff can describe every dish. Ask them about the wine list and confidence drops. Training takes time you don't have.
Your wine list isn't earning its keep
You know certain bottles aren't moving. You're not sure which pairings are driving covers or which gaps are costing you revenue.
Every sommelier costs what they cost
A full-time sommelier is a luxury many restaurants can't justify. But the experience they create — for guests and the team — is one every restaurant wants.

Entwine was built to solve all of this. Minimal investment. Maximum impact.

Here's what changes when you join Entwine.

Lightning-fast wine ordering

Order wine directly through the IMS — no more chasing suppliers or cross-referencing spreadsheets. Your order history, par levels, and supplier contacts in one place.

Wine list optimisation

Great wine lists aren't built on instinct. Entwine gives you the data to see what's selling, what's being paired, and what's missing — so you can build a list your guests actually want.

Inventory management

Track your inventory with complete confidence. Real-time stock levels, automatic low-stock alerts, and full visibility over what's moving and what isn't — all in one place.

Your guests' sommelier — at every table

When your restaurant goes live on Entwine, every diner can discover the perfect pairing for their dish — from your wine list, matched by our algorithm. Not generic. Yours.

How it works

01

Onboarding

Getting started is a team effort. We onboard your restaurant together — loading your food menu and wine list into your dedicated Entwine environment. We cross-reference every wine against our 8,000+ bottle database, and any wine we don't already have gets added by us with complete producer technical data. You're in good hands.

02

Pairing

Our ML algorithm pairs every wine to every dish — accounting for cooking method, sauces, and preparation. Pairings unique to your menu, not generic.

03

Launch

Your restaurant goes live on the Entwine app. Guests explore pairings at the table. You manage, order, and optimise from the IMS.

Your restaurant could be live on Entwine in days. Let's talk.
